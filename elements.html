Our Process

  • Design

    We define team goals on a decision matrix and design components with a focus on reliability, simplicity, and full vehicle understanding.

  • Design Review

    Throughout the design phase, design reviews offer an opportunity for scrutineering from other members, team alumni, and external suggestion. This aids in full car comprehension which allows are team to better integrate with other subteams and provides new members with great leaning experience.

  • Manufacturing

    We build the vehicle to from the ground up. This involves everything from writing software, welding the chassis, to precision machining. In house production allows the team to experience the relationship between engineering and manufacturability.

  • Testing

    We always aim to maximize testing during the spring semester. This allows us to test the current design for critical faults and gives us a benchmark towards further improvement of the car. Critical failures are then redesigned and manufactured in order to develop further reliability.
