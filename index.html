What SAE is All About!

The Formula SAE competitions challenge teams of university students to conceive, design, fabricate, develop, and compete with small, formula-style vehicles. The competitions give teams the chance to demonstrate and prove both their creativity and engineering skills in comparison to teams from other universities around the world

MEET THE TEAM

We are a relatively small team currently comprised of 14 members who are responsible for designing and manufacturing each of the six subsystems: Aerodynamics, Brakes and Ergonomics, Electronics, Chassis, Powertrain, and Suspension. Each of which will require members to practice good time management, research and development and engineering skills.

OUR GOALS

As a racing team, we seek to win competition, as a team of engineers, we seek to further our understanding of practical engineering, and as a team for businessmen, we aim to expand our professional network.

OUR GOALS BY SUBGROUPS

  • Powertrain

    • Design a reliable electronic fuel injection system
    • Learn engine management

  • Suspension

    • Design a light weight double wish bone suspension

  • Electronics

    • Improve our data acquisition systems
    • Learn engine management
    • Explore CAN Bus

  • Aerodynamics

    • Design lightweight carbon fiber body panels.
    • Maximize heat dissipation at critical areas.

  • Brake and Ergonomics

    • Dial in brake design
    • Design steering wheel

  • Chassis

    • Minimize weight while maximizing rigidity
    • Redesign rear end to accommodate for independent rear suspension
